Frequently Asked Questions
Preparing For Your Session
Do I need to prepare for a scanning session?
No special preparation is required. Just come with an open mind in a quiet and undisturbed environment.
What information do I need to provide before my session?
Only basic booking details like name and contact. The scanner connects intuitively with your timeline.
Is there anything I should or shouldn't do before a session?
Avoid alcohol or substances 24 hours prior. Being well-rested and meditating helps.
Booking & Logistics
Is there a fee for scanning sessions?
You’ll receive a donation link post-session to contribute any amount towards Art of Living's social projects.
How do I find an available scanner?
Use our portal to view available scanners. Book at least 1 hour ahead, or 1-2 weeks in advance for some.
What should I do if I need to cancel or reschedule?
Kindly inform KG via WhatsApp or phone well in advance, so the slot can be reassigned.
Privacy and Follow-up
Is my personal information kept confidential?
Yes, all information and session content is kept strictly confidential as per our privacy policy.
Can I repeat a scanning session?
Yes, but there should be at least a 30–45 day gap between sessions.
